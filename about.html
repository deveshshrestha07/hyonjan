About Us

Hyonjan Electrical Engineering Fabricator Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 1990, Hyonjan Electrical Engineering Fabricator Pvt. Ltd. is a leading name in Nepal's electrical engineering sector. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing electrical panel boards and conducting comprehensive electrical contracting jobs. Our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has solidified our position as the region's foremost electrical engineering provider.

Who Are We

With over three decades of excellence, Hyonjan Electrical has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality, defect-free goods and services on time and at competitive prices. Our commitment to customer satisfaction drives us to continuously enhance the efficacy of our products and services. Today, we proudly hold a major market share in Nepal for the fabrication of electrical control panels and contracting works, serving a wide range of applications including energy management and control.
