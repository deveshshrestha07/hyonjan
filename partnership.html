Partnership with AMETEK Power Instruments

Hyonjan Electrical Engineering Fabricator Pvt. Ltd. is proud to be the sole authorized distributor of AMETEK Power Instruments in Nepal. This partnership reflects the trust and reliability that Hyonjan Electrical has earned over the years. AMETEK, a renowned U.S.-based brand, is globally recognized for its high-quality electrical components, including MCBs, MCCBs, RCCBs, contractors, energy meters, and precision instruments. By representing AMETEK, we are able to offer our customers in Nepal the most reliable and customized solutions, ensuring superior performance and satisfaction. This collaboration enhances our ability to meet the diverse needs of our clients with world-class products and services.

What Do We Do?

01

Design & Fabrication

We specialize in the design and fabrication of high-quality electrical control panels and related products, tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

02

Consultancy

Our expert team provides comprehensive consultancy services, offering solutions that enhance the efficiency and reliability of electrical systems for various applications.

03

Marketing & Sales of Represented Brands

As the sole authorized distributor of AMETEK Power Instruments in Nepal, we handle the marketing and sales of internationally recognized electrical components and precision instruments, ensuring our customers have access to the best products in the industry.
